How are circles and triangles related?

Often trigonometry is taught in relation to triangles. sine and cosine just kind of magically show up in discussion without really explaining their relevance.

Short answer:

  • sin(θ) measures your vertical distance on a unit circle for the angle θ (the y value between -1 and 1)
  • cos(θ) measures your horizontal distance on a unit circle for the angle θ (the x value between -1 and 1)
